Instructions:
Note: The below operators must be between two numbers
  '+' - Addition
  '-' - Subtraction
  'x' or '*' - Multiplication
  'xx' or '**' - Power (Left number raised to the power of right number)
  '/' - Division (returns Quotient as Decimal)
  '//' - Division (returns Quotient as Integer)
  '.' - Enters a decimal to the input
  
  '(' - Open bracket
  ')' - Closed bracket
Note: If you wish to use brackets, then the expression must be inside the brackets
Example: 3+(2*5)
  
  '<' - Deletes the last entered text
  'Clear' - Clears the input screen
  '=' - Displays the result
  
Note: You can give your input through keyboard also
